Defensive postions

playing on the defensive line requires a player to be able to know his plays and also be able to be aggressive.

Shades:

when your lined up against the other teams offense, each player on the offense has a shade. the center's head is considered the 0. His left is considered the positive(or the plus side). His right is considered the negative (or the minus). The offense guard's shoulder closest to the center is considered the 1, his head is the 2, and his shoulder farthest from the center is his 3 shade. the offensive tackle's closest shoulder to the guard is the 4, head is 5, and other shoulder is the 6 etc.
